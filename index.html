about me

Max Yang is a 15-year-old student currently residing in San Diego, California, although he was originally born in Lake Zurich, Illinois. He is a 10th grader attending Canyon Crest Academy. Max is passionate about competitive programming and enjoys developing various projects that align with his interests, including this website. In addition to his programming skills, Max is also an accomplished violinist.

music

Max Yang has been playing the violin for 12 years, showcasing his talent in various youth orchestras. He has performed at prestigious venues such as the Rady Shell in Jacobs Park and even ventured across the border to perform in Tijuana. Max's dedication to the violin was recognized when he clinched third place in the American Protégé International Piano & Strings Competition at Carnegie Hall.

volunteering

AYLUS, also known as the Alliance of Youth Leaders in the United States, is an organization that coordinates and participates in various volunteer opportunities with the goal of spreading goodwill to others. I genuinely enjoy volunteering at Aylus, not just for college applications, but because I truly believe in its impact. Whether I'm helping low-income families at the local food bank or organizing beach cleanups to protect wildlife, every volunteer opportunity feels like a meaningful contribution to the world. Additionally, I teach kids math, primarily using Beast Academy, and English both online and in person. Currently I am part of the leadership for AYLUS San Diego Due to my dedication to volunteering, I was honored with a Silver Presidential Volunteer Service Award for my efforts in 2023.
